Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services, tutors, and scheduling.

What subjects do you cover?

We cover all core academic subjects from middle school through university level, including AP/IB preparation. Our specialties include Advanced Mathematics (Calculus, Algebra, Statistics), Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics), English Language Arts, and standardized test prep (SAT, ACT).

How do I choose the right tutor?

Our academic coordinators use a detailed matching process based on your student's needs, learning style, and personality. You can also view our tutor profiles in the "Meet Our Certified Experts" section to learn about their background and specialties before requesting a session.

Are sessions only held online?

While most of our sessions are conducted online through our interactive virtual classroom, we offer in-person tutoring in select metropolitan areas. Please contact us to check for in-person availability in your region.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a minimum of 24 hours notice for any session cancellation or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may result in a charge for the full session time, as our tutors reserve that time specifically for you.

How do payments work?

We offer flexible payment options including pay-per-session, monthly packages, and semester plans. All major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers are accepted. You'll receive an invoice after each session with detailed breakdown of charges.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for group sessions and family packages. For groups of 3 or more students, you'll receive a 15% discount. Additionally, families with multiple students receive a 10% discount on all sessions.
